How to Play Battleship

• Your 5 ships are automatically placed on your board

• Take turns firing at coordinates on opponent's board

• Hit = Red X (you hit an enemy ship)

• Miss = Blue dot (empty water)

• Win by sinking all enemy ships first!

• Ships: Carrier(5), Battleship(4), Cruiser(3), Submarine(3), Destroyer(2)
